IC Design & Fabrication (TSMC 65nm)

As part of the EEE D2 Integrated Circuit Design exercise at the University of Southampton, I collaborated with a team of 6 to design and implement a TSMC 65nm Node Integrated Circuit. This project involved the complete IC design flow, from schematic capture and simulation (using S-Edit and T-Spice) to mask level layout and design rule checking (using L-Edit and Calibre DRC/LVS). Our team successfully validated the design through extensive simulations and submitted the final GDSII files for fabrication.
